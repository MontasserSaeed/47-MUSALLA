About Us

Dar-e-arqaam is a musalla located in 101-47 Thorncliffe Pk Dr. We facilitate daily 5 times prayer along with Jumaa prayer. We do provide different programs to help community to learn Islamic values and principles.

We strive to make positive impact on our community to help and equip them with Islamic teachings. We work together with other islamic mosques and musalla to coordinate and be part of our muslim network.
